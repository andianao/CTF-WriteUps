Plan

sending the password to the localhost

Syntax: echo "Enter-your-message-here" | nc <ip address or hostname> <port number> ( write without the < > )

$ echo "MU4VWeTyJk8ROof1qqmcBPaLh7lDCPvS" | nc bandit.labs.overthewire.org 30000
